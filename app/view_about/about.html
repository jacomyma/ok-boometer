About OK Boometer

This website measures OK Boomer on Twitter. We count one OK-booming for each tweet that is replying to another tweet, or retweeting with a comment, where the tweet contains "OK Boomer" (and minor variations like "OK, boomer" etc.) but not more than 20 characters, URLs and mentions (@someone) excluded. We do not count retweets or likes. You have to OK-boom by yourself.

This is just a socio-technical experiment. Is the avocado the new rotten tomato? Who knows.

How to use: Watch the OK Boomings live, or check if someone is the boomer you had always suspected.
